Our Story

Founded in 2022, FLOW began with a simple mission: to create water bottles that combine minimalist design with maximum functionality.

We believe that staying hydrated shouldn't mean compromising on style. Every bottle is thoughtfully designed to fit seamlessly into your daily life, whether you're at the office, the gym, or on an adventure.

Our commitment to sustainability drives everything we do. Each FLOW bottle is made from premium materials that are built to last, reducing the need for single-use plastics.
